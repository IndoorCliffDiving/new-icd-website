What We Offer

Training Sessions

From beginner to pro, you're on the right platform with us. Come join us on Tuesdays and Sundays for Skill Classes and Open Training sessions.

A Skill Class is a lesson where the trainer guides you and teaches you various diving skills. There are Skill Classes for beginners, intermediates, and pros. The level of the class is indicated in the title of the Skill Class.

The Open Training sessions are characterized by free diving, where acrobatic athletes challenge each other and learn from one another. A trainer is always present for questions and tips, but you must decide for yourself what you want to work on. These sessions are open to all levels, but some level of independence is required.

Competitions

Show what you're made of during our competitions. We organize events for X-Diving, High Diving, and the Splash Dive contest. Or join our coaches for national and international tournaments.

Trips

Join one of our Cliff Dive vacations or training camps to the most beautiful spots in Europe. Guided by experienced Cliff Divers, so you can focus on your most awesome dive ever.

Private Bookings

Also great for company outings, children's parties, clinics, bachelor parties, extra school activities, resilience training, anti-acrophobia lessons, water spectacle sports day, or other group outings.

Why Indoor Cliff Diving

Always watched in awe those cool guys and girls jumping off cliffs while you're sunbathing on the beach? Don't just lie there, it's easier than you think! Jumping, diving, flipping, or just spinning; you can start at your own level in the tower pool of Sloterparkbad. Find your challenge somewhere between 0 and 10 meters and we'll teach you how to safely dive into the water.

Need more of a challenge? Maybe you are that cool chick or dude who wants to learn a new dive. Then you're on the right platform with us too! Challenge yourself and with the professional guidance of, among others, the Dutch champion on the ten-meter platform, you'll be shining on top of the cliff in your speedo this summer!
